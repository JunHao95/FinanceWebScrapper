    1. Extract highs, lows, closes, opens, volumes as numpy arrays

    4. POC = bin_centers[argmax(volume_by_bin)]
    5. Value area: sort bins descending by volume, accumulate until >= 0.70 * total_volume; VAH = max center in set, VAL = min center in set
